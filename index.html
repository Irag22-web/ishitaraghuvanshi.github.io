About Me

Hi! I’m Ishita. I am a Master's student at Georgia Tech studying Biomedical Engineering. I love planning, designing, and creating thoughtful, human-centered solutions.

In my free time, I love painting and drawing, spending quality time with family over board games, and experimenting with new dishes in the kitchen. I’m also almost always planning an event—whether it’s an intimate Italian night or organizing large-scale cultural celebrations like Diwali events for over a thousand people. These experiences fuel my creativity, organization, and love for bringing people together.
